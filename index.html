About Us

Embrace

Embrace is a platform that can be used to combat loneliness between all generations, especially because of the pandemic. The service is split into two services: an app that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to match seniors to volunteers, and an extended reality platform for young generations. After the app matches volunteers with seniors, there's a built-in calling feature that can be used by the volunteers to check-in with the seniors.

My Services

Mobile App

Embrace offers a mobile app that can connect seniors with volunteers to facilitate check-ins to ensure that seniors don't get lonely during the pandemic.

Extended Reality

Embrace is expanding its services to offer an extended reality platform to help young people connect globally. We hope to connect people to help them deal with their mental health.
